8.8 [] and ()

<..latex,sty math del..>
   \def\({\leavevmode\st:math}
   \def\){\ed:math}
 \NewConfigure{()}[2]{\def\st:math{#1}\def\ed:math{#2}}
   \def\[{\st:Math}
   \def\]{\ed:Math}
 \NewConfigure{[]}[2]{\def\st:Math{#1}\def\ed:Math{#2}}
 \Configure{()}{$}{$}
 \Configure{[]}{$$}{$$}
 -_-_-

<..amsmath.sty..>
 \expandafter\def\csname [ \endcsname{\st:Math}
 \expandafter\def\csname ] \endcsname{\ed:Math}
