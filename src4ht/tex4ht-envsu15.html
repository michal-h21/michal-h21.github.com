  • A Netpbm-based script (src)
     Ggs -sDEVICE=ppm -sOutputFile=zz%%4.ppm -q -dbatch -dNOPAUSE zz%%4.ps -c quit  
     Gmv zz%%4.ppm yz%%4.ppm
