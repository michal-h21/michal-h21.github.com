1.10.1 Examples

General Format:

<table:table table:name="..." table:style-name="tbl">  
  <table:table-column table:style-name="..."/>... <table:table-column/>  
  <table:table-row table:name="...">  
    <table:table-cell> <text:p>...</text:p> </table:table-cell>  
    ....  
  </table:table-row>  
  .....  
</table:table>

May include subtables

<table:sub-table>  
  .......  
</table:sub-table>
