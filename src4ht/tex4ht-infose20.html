 \Configure{epsfig}...........................#1
 
    #1  before

    In LaTeX, epsfig reduces to graphicsx
 
    Example:
 
     \Configure{epsfig}
        {\Configure{graphics}
           {} {\xdef\foo{width="\the\Gin@req@width"
               height="\the\Gin@req@height"}}%
         \Picture+[epsfig]{}}
        {\EndPicture\immediate\write16{..... \foo}}
