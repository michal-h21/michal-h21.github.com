1.20.8 Page Breaks

<..configure ooffice tex4ht..>+
 \OOstyles{%
   <style:style style:name="clearpage" style:family="paragraph"
       style:parent-style-name="Text-body">
       <style:paragraph-properties  fo:break-before="page" />
   </style:style>
 }
 \def\Hclearpage{%
   \par \ifvmode
      \IgnorePar\EndP
      \HCode{<.new page.>}%
      \ShowPar\par
   \fi
 }
-_-_-

<..new page..>
 <text:p text:style-name="clearpage"></text:p>%
-_-_-
