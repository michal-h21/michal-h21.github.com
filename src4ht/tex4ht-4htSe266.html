What is the difference between ‘\uccode‘\~\count255 %SPACE’ and

      \uccode‘\~\count255% 
      \uccode‘\u‘\u% 

utf8x.def has both.

The above is to preserve characters in ‘sensitive’ locations:

\documentclass{article} 
\usepackage{ucs} 
\usepackage[utf8]{inputenc} 
\usepackage{ngerman} 
\usepackage{hyperref} 
 
\begin{document} 
   X \autoref{fig:add_todo} 
 
   \begin{figure}[hbt!] 
      \caption{Fr X} 
      \label{fig:add_todo} 
   \end{figure} 
\end{document}
