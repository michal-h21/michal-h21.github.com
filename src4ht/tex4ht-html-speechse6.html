0.6 Bibliography and Equation References

<..configure html-speech latex..>+
 \def\@cite#1#2{%
    \HCode{\add:description{added-description}{\a:speak:citation}}%
    {#1\if@tempswa , #2\fi}}
 \def\@biblabel#1{%
    \HCode{\add:description{added-description}{\a:speak:bib}}%
    #1}
