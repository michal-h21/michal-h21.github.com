The following two sample codes provide different behavior.

\documentclass[12pt]{article}  
\usepackage{makeidx}  
\makeindex  
\begin{document}  
\index{Hola: Hallo} Hola  
\printindex  
\end{document}

\documentclass{article}  
\usepackage[frenchb]{babel}  
\usepackage{makeidx}  
\makeindex  
 
\begin{document}  
\begin{itemize}  
\item \index{blabla} blabla  
\item blabla  
\end{itemize}  
\end{document}
