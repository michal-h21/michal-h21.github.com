Superscripts

The following command ‘\def\@textsuperscript#1{% {\m@th\ensuremath{^{\mbox{\fontsize\sf@size\z@#1}}}}}’, where the exponent is too early to be an active character.

<..latex ltfloat..>+
 \def\:temp#1{%
   {\m@th\ensuremath{^{\mbox{\fontsize\sf@size\z@#1}}}}}
 \ifx \@textsuperscript\:temp
   \def\:tempc#1{{\m@th
      \ifmmode {\HCode{}}\sp {\mbox{\fontsize\sf@size\z@#1}}%
      \else
         \a:textsuperscript
            {\mbox{#1}}\b:textsuperscript
      \fi }}
   \HLet\@textsuperscript\:tempc
 \fi
 \NewConfigure{textsuperscript}{2}
 \Configure{textsuperscript}
    {$\relax{\HCode{}}\sp}
    {$}
 -_-_-

The definition of ‘\@textsuperscript’ in latex2e uses feartures not available in latex209.
