1.1 Subscrips and Superscripts (sub and super)

<..configure jsml-math tex4ht..>+
 <.sub, sup, subsup.>
 <.limits sub, sup, subsup.>
-_-_-

<..sub, sup, subsup..>
 \Configure{SUB}
    {\Send{BACK}
      {<.start msub base.>}<.msub sub.>}
    {<.close msub.>}
 \Configure{SUP}
    {\Send{BACK}
    {<.start msup base.>}<.msup super.>}
    {<.close msup.>}
 \Configure{SUBSUP}
    {\Send{BACK}
    {<.start msubsup base.>}<.msubsup sub.>}
    {<.msubsup sup.>}
    {<.close msubsup.>}
-_-_-
